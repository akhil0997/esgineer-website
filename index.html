THE ESGINEER PROTOCOL

Cloud ESG Platforms Are a
Compliance Liability.

When your emissions data, production records, and supply chain figures sit on a third-party server, you lose control of your most sensitive operational intelligence — and your audit trail.

🔓

Your Data on Their Infrastructure

Cloud ESG vendors store your Scope 1 production volumes, energy consumption, and Scope 3 supplier data on shared multi-tenant servers. A breach, subpoena, or vendor acquisition puts your competitive intelligence at risk without your knowledge.

RISK FACTOR
⚠️

Audit Trails You Cannot Certify

When a regulator or statutory auditor asks for data lineage, can you prove your emissions records were never altered? With cloud platforms, your audit trail lives outside your firewall in a system your team does not own, control, or version.

AUDIT RISK
🏛️

ESGineer: Air-Gapped by Architecture

The ESGineer computation engine runs entirely on your servers using SQLite and DuckDB. Every calculation, every report, every audit log entry is written locally — signed by your system, owned by your organisation. Data sovereignty is not a feature; it is the architecture.
